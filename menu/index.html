Appetizers

  • Seafood Stuffed Mushrooms

    Fresh mushrooms loaded with our signature seafood stuffing, then smothered in Monterey Jack cheese and baked until bubbly brown. We dare you to have just one.

  • *RAW OR STEAM OYSTERS

    6 or 12 oysters. Served with Boom Boom sauce.

  • White Wine and Roasted Garlic Mussels

    Tender mussels steamed in white wine with roasted garlic, roasted tomatoes and green onions. Served with grilled artisan bread for soaking up all that delicious broth.

  • Langostino Lobster-Artichoke-and-Seafood Dip

    An addictive combo of langostino lobster, seafood, artichokes, spinach and three cheeses, plus house-made pico de gallo and warm tortilla chips on the side.

  • Signature Shrimp Cocktail

    These plump, tender shrimp are cooked and chilled in-house every day. Dunk them in our classic cocktail sauce and taste the difference.
